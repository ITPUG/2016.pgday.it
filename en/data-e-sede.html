Date and location

We are absolutely delighted to officially announce the date and location of the tenth edition of the Italian PGDay:

On Tuesday, December 13, 2016 the Camera di Commercio di Prato will host the annual edition of our favourite conference.

We like to point out that this is the tenth edition of the Italian PGDay!

As ITPUG, we want to thank in advance all the volunteers who work to make the conference possible.

Please remember to read the Code of conduct to ensure everyone has a wonderful conference!
